My name is Jinsu Hong, and I'm a Ph.D. student at Georgia State University under the supervision of Dr. Berkay Aydin. My research focuses on Data Science applications in Space Weather, specifically machine learning for solar event forecasting, image processing for object detection, and data integration. I'm passionate about understanding the complexities of space weather and its impact on our planet.
