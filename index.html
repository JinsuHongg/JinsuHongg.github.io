Hello! I am Jinsu Hong, a Computer Science Ph.D. student at Georgia State University advised by Dr. Berkay Aydin. My research bridges artificial intelligence and space weather, focusing on advanced machine learning techniques for solar flare and event forecasting. My work tackles complex challenges in the field, including managing highly imbalanced data, utilizing computer vision for object detection, and advancing uncertainty quantification. Ultimately, my goal is to build reliable, data-driven forecasting models that enable critical decision-making and create a lasting, positive impact on technology and society.
